### Introduction

In the competitive world of web development, standing out from the crowd is
crucial. One powerful way to captivate your audience and elevate your website's
visual appeal is by using
[CSS gradients](https://developer.mozilla.org/en-US/docs/Web/CSS/gradient). n
this blog post, we will explore how you can apply gradients to borders, text
color and SVGs to create stunning effects that will leave a lasting impression
on your visitors.

### What are CSS gradients?

CSS gradients are a smooth transition between two or more colors.They can be
[linear](https://developer.mozilla.org/en-US/docs/Web/CSS/gradient/linear-gradient),
[radial](https://developer.mozilla.org/en-US/docs/Web/CSS/gradient/radial-gradient),
or
[conic](https://developer.mozilla.org/en-US/docs/Web/CSS/gradient/conic-gradient)
offering diverse design possibilities.
